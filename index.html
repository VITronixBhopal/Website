About Us

Our Club is formed on the foundations to solve the real time problems in Indian Society in the fields of defense, military, country security, pollution, surveillance, renewable energy, robotics, autonomous systems, Internet of things, internal working of complex systems, etc. It also gives a platform for deserving young engineering minds in developing the promising and sustainable solutions for the betterment of the society. This club will encompass support not only from the elite faculty, at the campus, and also some experts from industrial domain to help and create a collaborative and sustaining environment for the members of the club. This club also encourages everyone to share ideas, knowledge, and technology and work towards solving daily life technical problems. This club holds various workshops, symposiums, events, science fairs, technical presentations, ideation challenges, findings, with the students. Our vision is to create a better society and get better advancements in the field of technology.
